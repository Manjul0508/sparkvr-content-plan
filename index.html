30 Days Content Calendar

This content calendar starts from 18 June 2026 and covers 30 days. The plan includes 20 social media posts, 5 short reels, and 5 website blogs.

20 Social media posts
5 Short reels
5 Website blogs

Plan Overview

Simple direction for the month

Goal

To make more schools aware of SparkVR and explain how VR can help students learn better.

Audience

School owners, principals, teachers, parents, and students from classes 5th to 10th.

Style

Clean, bright, school-friendly, and easy to understand with simple words and clear visuals.

Reference Brands

Competitor content direction

Brand What They Do What SparkVR Can Use Our Direction
Extramarks @extramarks Shares education content, student-focused posts, and learning-related communication. Keep the content easy for parents, students, and schools to understand. SparkVR should keep the message simple and show how VR makes learning clearer.
FotonVR @fotonvr Focuses on VR learning, product use, and classroom technology. Show more product use, VR lesson examples, and school demo moments. SparkVR should focus on school workshops, student experience, and real classroom value.
SparkVR @sparkvr.in Offers 3D and 360° VR learning content for students from classes 5th to 10th. Use simple posts, reels, blogs, and founder-led trust content. Build awareness, explain benefits, show demos, and encourage school enquiries.

Content Focus

What we will talk about

VR Learning

Show how students can understand topics better with 3D and 360° learning.

Classroom Benefits

Explain how SparkVR can help teachers explain difficult topics in a simple way.

School Enquiries

Share content that encourages schools to ask about demos, workshops, and VR lessons.
