30 Days Content Calendar

This content calendar starts from 18 June 2026 and covers 30 days. The plan includes 20 social media posts/carousels, 5 short reels, 5 website blogs, and 2 influencer content pieces per month. Reels can be created using stock visuals, motion graphics, text animation, AI visuals, and brand assets.

20 Social posts / carousels
5 Short reels
5 Website blogs
2 Influencer content / month

Plan Overview

Simple direction for the month

Goal

To make more schools aware of SparkVR and explain how VR can help students understand topics better.

Audience

School owners, principals, teachers, parents, and students from classes 5th to 10th.

Style

Clean, bright, school-friendly, easy to understand, and possible to create without live shoots.

Reference Brands

Competitor content direction

Brand What They Do What SparkVR Can Use Our Direction
Extramarks @extramarks Shares education content, student-focused posts, and learning-related communication. Keep the content easy for parents, students, and schools to understand. SparkVR should keep the message simple and show how VR makes learning clearer.
FotonVR @fotonvr Focuses on VR learning, product use, and classroom technology. Show more product use, VR lesson examples, and school demo-style content. SparkVR should focus on visual learning, student experience, and real classroom value.

Content Focus

What we will talk about

VR Learning

Show how students can understand topics better with 3D and 360° learning.

Classroom Benefits

Explain how SparkVR can help teachers explain difficult topics in a simple way.

School Enquiries

Share content that encourages schools to ask about demos, workshops, and VR lessons.
